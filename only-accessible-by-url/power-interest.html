Key insight
Risk exposure is asymmetric and inverse to formal power. Stakeholders in the lower-right quadrant — tail-end irrigators, First Nations, environmental holders — bear the greatest operational consequences from AI failure yet hold the least formal governance authority over system design. This structural asymmetry is the central equity problem the system must address through co-design and tail-node performance parity.
